Summary
We, a group of Year 1 students from CamEd Business School (Group L), are seeking sponsorship for our charity campaign, “The Smile of Cambodian Children.” “SCC”
This event will take place over 3 days in Siem Reap Province, Cambodia, aiming to support underprivileged children by providing essential educational materials. Our campaign focuses on helping children continue their education and build a brighter future.


Vision
To build a caring community where people from different backgrounds come together to support and uplift children in need.
We aim to give underprivileged children a better start in life by providing access to essential learning materials. Within one month, we plan to support 270 students from different schools, helping them continue their education with confidence.


Mission
Our mission is to raise funds to support children in rural areas who lack access to basic educational resources.
We are committed to:
* Promoting equal education opportunities
* Providing essential school supplies
* Inspiring hope and motivation in young learners
We believe that education should not be a privilege, but a right accessible to every child.


Expected Impact (Added Section)
Through this campaign, we expect to:
* Provide school supplies (books, pens, bags) to 270 students
* Improve students’ motivation and school attendance
* Strengthen community awareness about education inequality

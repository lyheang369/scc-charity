## What is Smile of Cambodian Children (SCC)?

Smile of Cambodian Children, known as SCC, is a student-led charity campaign organized by Year 1 students of CamEd Business School. The campaign's full name is "The Smile of Cambodian Children." Its mission is to raise funds and collect school supplies for underprivileged children living in rural Cambodia who lack access to basic educational resources.

SCC believes that education is a right, not a privilege — and that no child should be left behind because of their circumstances.

---

## Who organizes SCC?

SCC is organized by a group of Year 1 students from CamEd Business School in Cambodia. The campaign is supported by CamEd Business School as an institution.

- **Organized by:** Smile of Cambodian Children (SCC) — CamEd Business School Year 1 students
- **Supported by:** CamEd Business School

## What is the goal of the campaign?

The campaign has two main goals:

1. **Raise funds** to purchase essential school supplies for children in need.
2. **Deliver those supplies directly** to underprivileged children in rural Cambodia.

The target is to support **270+ students** from multiple schools in Siem Reap Province. Every dollar raised goes directly to educational materials — there is no overhead cost taken from donations.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is SCC a registered NGO?**
SCC is a student-led charity campaign organized by CamEd Business School Year 1 students. It operates as a campaign rather than a registered non-governmental organization.

**Q: How much of my donation actually reaches the children?**
100% of funds raised go directly to purchasing educational materials for children. There are no overhead costs deducted from donations.

**Q: Can I donate from outside Cambodia?**
Yes. Contact SCC via email at support@scc-charity.com or Telegram at @thearysrms to arrange an international donation. The team will personally guide you through the process.

**Q: What is KHQR?**
KHQR is Cambodia's national QR code payment standard used by most Cambodian banks and payment apps. Donors with a Cambodian bank account or app can scan the SCC KHQR code to donate instantly.

**Q: When did SCC start?**
SCC is a campaign for the 2026 academic year, organized by CamEd Business School Year 1 students.

**Q: What is CamEd Business School?**
CamEd Business School is a business school in Cambodia. Its Year 1 students organized the Smile of Cambodian Children charity campaign to give back to underprivileged communities in rural Cambodia.

**Q: Where exactly is the charity event happening?**
The main outreach activity takes place at Daun Chrom Primary School, Svay Chek Village, Keo Pur Commune, Puok District, Siem Reap Province, Cambodia on Saturday 23 May 2026.

**Q: How many children will SCC help?**
SCC aims to support 270 or more underprivileged children across multiple schools in Siem Reap Province.
